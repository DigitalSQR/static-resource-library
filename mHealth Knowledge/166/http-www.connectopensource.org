<h1 style="padding-top: 25px;">CONNECT is an open source project designed to enable the secure, effective exchange of information.&nbsp;</h1>
<p>CONNECT implements the Nationwide Health Information Network (NwHIN) standards and specifications, including Direct project specifications, and offers organizations a robust, scalable platform to exchange health data with their partners, share medical information and improve patient care.</p>
